What ProofCam does

ProofCam adds a date, time, GPS location, address, and an optional note onto your photos using your device's clock, GPS, and connection. It is a record-keeping tool for your own use.

Accuracy — credible, not official proof

Stamp details depend on your device clock, GPS signal strength, and whether you are online. Location and addresses can be approximate, and time depends on your device settings. ProofCam is meant for your own records. Do not rely on a ProofCam stamp as legal, official, or guaranteed proof of where or when a photo was taken.
